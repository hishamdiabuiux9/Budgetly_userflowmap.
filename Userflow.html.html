Trust Architecture: Budgetly never asks for bank login credentials or passwords. This is the single most important trust message in the app for the Egyptian market.

Emotional Design: The score screen is the most emotionally charged in the app. The ring animation (0 → score value, 1s ease-out) creates anticipation. Color coding is instant feedback: green = calm, yellow = caution, red = urgency. Deep-linking: Each "At Risk" tip card links directly to the relevant screen (e.g., "You have 3 unreviewed subscriptions" → taps directly into Subscription Detection flow).
